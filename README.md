# OneDW – AI-Powered Hyperlocal Home Services Platform

OneDW is an AI-powered hyperlocal home services platform designed to connect customers with trusted and verified local service professionals through a secure, transparent, and user-friendly digital ecosystem. The platform addresses the challenges faced by people in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities, where finding reliable electricians, plumbers, carpenters, cleaners, and other skilled workers is often difficult due to the absence of an organized digital marketplace.

The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence to intelligently match customers with the most suitable professionals based on their location, experience, availability, ratings, and customer reviews. By using AI-driven recommendations, OneDW reduces the time required to search for skilled workers while improving service quality and customer satisfaction. The application supports multiple booking channels, including a responsive web application, WhatsApp integration, and a multilingual voice assistant, making it accessible even to users with limited technical knowledge.

To build a secure and scalable platform, OneDW uses **React.js** with **Material UI** for the frontend, **FastAPI (Python)** for backend services, and **MongoDB Atlas** for cloud database management. The platform integrates the **Google Gemini Flash** model for AI-powered recommendations and Natural Language Processing (NLP) for voice interactions. Secure user authentication is implemented using **JWT (JSON Web Tokens)**, while **OpenStreetMap** provides location-based service discovery. The project is version-controlled using **Git & GitHub** and deployed using **Vercel** and **Render**.

OneDW emphasizes trust and transparency through verified worker profiles, customer ratings, reviews, AI-assisted price estimation, and real-time booking updates. Beyond helping customers, the platform empowers local professionals by increasing their digital visibility, generating consistent employment opportunities, and supporting sustainable income growth.

The project aims to promote digital inclusion by offering an easy-to-use, AI-enabled solution that simplifies home service booking for customers while creating a reliable employment ecosystem for skilled workers. Future enhancements include online payment integration, live service tracking, AI chatbot support, subscription-based services, predictive demand analysis, and an advanced admin dashboard for monitoring platform performance and user engagement.
